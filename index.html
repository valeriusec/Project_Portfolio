About Me

Hi there! I'm Valeriu, a front-end development enthusiast currently living in Italy. At just 21 years old, I'm still a student learning the ropes in this exciting field. Before discovering my passion for technology, I worked as a manual laborer in a warehouse. But I always had a desire to work in the computer world, and it was this ambition that led me to pursue a career in front-end development.

I've always had a passion for creating and designing digital experiences. My goal is to build intuitive, visually appealing, and user-friendly websites and applications. I'm constantly learning and developing my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various front-end frameworks such as React. I have a strong desire to stay up-to-date with the latest web development trends and technologies to provide the best solutions for my clients in the future.

As a student, I've been taking on small projects and working on personal projects to gain practical experience and build my portfolio. I'm always eager to learn and take on new challenges.

I'm excited to continue my journey in the world of front-end development and can't wait to bring my skills, passion, and enthusiasm to future projects.
